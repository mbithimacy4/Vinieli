Services

DENTAL SERVICES
OFFERED

Tooth extraction- careful and tactful removal of a damaged tooth that is beyond repair.

Teeth cleaning- Removal of teeth stains(tartar/calculus) that have accumulated over a period of time.

Teeth whitening- Chemical in-office lightening of the teeth.

Permanent fillings- These are done on teeth that have cavities that may present with sensitivity.

Root Canal Treatment- A process where the tooth's nerve endings are removed. Done to save infected painful teeth from extraction.

Teeth Replacement- This is done after one has lost a tooth. It can either be permanent(crown and bridge) or removable(dentures).

Orthodontic Treatment- This is done to correct teeth malocclusion. It includes fixed braces and other removable appliances.

Minor Oral Surgery- Disimpactions for abnormally grown wisdom teeth and canines, Drainage of abscesses, Suturing and Cutting of tongue and lip frenulum.
